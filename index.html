World Overview

In 2021, 98 countries increased their spending. (Out of 138 countries with available data)

and 36 countries decreased their spending.

The top 10 spending countries accounted for ~75% of world's total. That is about 1.6 Trillion US Dollars.

Adding the next 10, top 20 countries account for ~86% of the total.

North America has the most regional total. US = $800B. Canada=$26B

East Asia ranks 2nd in regional total. $410B

Western Europe ranks 3rd in regional total. $305B

Middle East ranks 4th in regional total. $186B

Since WWII, East Asia has been an important region in preventing the spread of communism led by U.S and its Major Non-Nato Allies.
