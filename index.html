Out of 138 countries with available data, 98 countries increased their spending in 2021 from 2020.

and 36 countries decreased their spending in 2021 from 2020.

The top 10 spending countries accounted for 75% of world's total.

Adding the next 10, top 20 countries account for 86% of the total. (Out of 138 Countries with data available)

Besides North America, Asia & Oceania has the most regional total. Accounting for ~27.5% of world's total.

Presense of geopolitical interests from countries like China, U.S, India, Japan, Taiwan, South and North Korea and Russia, makes this region one of the hot bed of possible military conflict.

One of the major power in the region, China ranks as the world's second largest spender on military expenditure.

It has been increasing the expense since 1994 for 27 consecutive years.

According to Arms Transfer data, China is one of the biggest importer of Russian Arms. And politically also has close ties to Russia.

India is the third largest spender in the world and has been increasing the spending every year since 2012.
