World Overview

- 2nd in Military Expenditure

- Formal Ally: North Korea
- Nuclear Warheads

Japan, South Korea & Taiwan
- 3rd, 10th, ~20th in GDP

After the fall of Soviet Union, Europe went through a period of demilitarization. Since the annexation of Crimea by Russia, Europe has been steadily increasing military spending once again.

The upward trend was already there. Most of the Europe increased their spending in 2021 (marked red) even before the Russian invasion of Ukraine in 2022. This prompted Sweden and Finland to newly join NATO alliance, but a resolution in Ukraine still seems far away at the time of writing.

The rearmament trend in Europe will likely continue.
